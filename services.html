Services

Our Services

Manicure

The manicure service at Code Style Beauty Salon focuses on nail and hand care. It includes nail trimming, shaping, cuticle care, exfoliation, moisturization, and polishing. Benefits include improved nail health and enhanced appearance. The service is customizable, with pricing based on the type of manicure and additional treatments. Clients receive aftercare recommendations for maintaining their nails and hands.

Hair

The hair service at Code Style Beauty Salon encompasses treatments and procedures aimed at enhancing hair health, appearance, and style. It includes consultation, hair analysis, treatments like haircutting, shampooing, conditioning, styling, and coloring. The service is tailored to each client's needs, promoting improved hair health and appearance. Pricing varies based on treatments and salon factors. Aftercare recommendations ensure clients maintain results.

Facial

The facial service at Code Style Beauty Salon involves cleansing, exfoliating, and nourishing the skin to promote a healthy and rejuvenated complexion. It includes steam, extractions, mask application, and moisturizing. enefits include improved skin texture, hydration, and a radiant glow. Pricing varies based on the type of facial and additional treatments. Aftercare advice ensures clients maintain skin health and results.
